## Sprint Ceremonies

### Sprint Planning (2 hours)
**When:** First Monday of sprint
**Who:** Entire team
**Agenda:**
1. Review previous sprint results
2. Present upcoming sprint goals
3. Review and estimate tickets
4. Assign tickets to team members
5. Identify dependencies and risks
6. Set sprint commitments

### Daily Standup (15 minutes)
**When:** Every weekday at 9:30 AM
**Who:** Development team
**Format:**
- Round-robin updates (2 min each)
- Blockers discussion
- Quick monitoring review

### Sprint Review (1 hour)
**When:** Last Friday of sprint
**Who:** Team + stakeholders
**Agenda:**
1. Demo completed features

### Sprint Retrospective (30 minutes)

**Who:** Team only

- Estimate complexity

## Risk Management

### Sprint 1 Risks
- **Risk:** ML research takes longer than expected
  - **Mitigation:** Extend to Sprint 2 if needed
- **Risk:** WebSocket architecture too complex
  - **Mitigation:** Start with simple design, iterate

### Sprint 2 Risks
- **Risk:** WebSocket implementation challenges
  - **Mitigation:** Allocate full 2 weeks, have backup plan
- **Risk:** ML performance issues
  - **Mitigation:** Keep rule-based as fallback

### Sprint 3 Risks
- **Risk:** Mobile development delays
  - **Mitigation:** MVP approach, defer non-critical features
- **Risk:** Resource constraints
  - **Mitigation:** Prioritize critical path items

### Sprint 4 Risks
- **Risk:** App store approval delays
  - **Mitigation:** Submit early, plan for revisions
- **Risk:** Performance optimization challenges
  - **Mitigation:** Start with quick wins, iterate
